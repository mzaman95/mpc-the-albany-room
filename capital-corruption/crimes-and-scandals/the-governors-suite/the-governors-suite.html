Governor Samuel J. Tilden

25th Governor of NY (1875-1876), an anti-corruption leader who took on “Boss” Tweed and Tammany Hall.

Governor William Sulzer

39th Governor of NY (1913-1913), accused of diverting campaign funds, is the only NY governor to be impeached and removed from office.

Governor David A. Paterson

55th Governor of NY (2008-2010), his political career was cut short due to allegations that he unlawfully solicited free New York Yankee tickets and lied under oath about the incident.

Governor Eliot Spitzer

54th Governor of NY (2007-2008), Eliot Spitzer resigned in disgrace after it was revealed he was patronizing prostitutes (actions that extended from his time as NY Attorney General).

Governor Andrew Cuomo

56th Governor of NY (2011-2021), Andrew Cuomo was found by the Attorney General’s Office to have abused his political power by sexually harassing at least eleven women while in office.

Lt. Governor Brian Benjamin

Lt. Governor of NY (2021-2022), he resigned after being indicted for abusing his authority while a NY Senator by procuring a state-funded grant in exchange for campaign contributions.
